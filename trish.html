Trish Thùy Trang (Trang, Nguyễn Thùy) was born in 1980 in Ho Chi Minh City.
Her family immigrated to the United States, settling in Sugarland, Texas before moving to Southern California.
She signed under Asia Entertainment in 1997, performing for their variety shows.
She released three albums under them before going independent in 2008.
She independently released three albums (some songs written and produced while at Asia).
Her last album, Whisper was released in 2010, marking the end of her music career since then.
