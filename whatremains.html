What Remains Unknown?

Photos, specifically photos hosted on Photobucket and other defunct or overhauled websites are lost to time unless those host links were archived as well. The gaps that missing photos pose are context for advice based posts, graphic design trends, information on potential illnesses.
Without photos, advice based posts loses visuals that can otherwise help others with the same issues solve their problems with.
Without photos, users lose individuality from the loss of avatars and signatures. These graphics showed passion and love for the hamsters they raised, and was supposed to immortalize their hamsters on the internet.

The systems that failed to preserve these images were:

  1. The host website itself, Hamster Hideout
  2. The image hosting websites
Due to the way images were displayed on forums, the user doesn't directly upload images to the website, but rather embed a link to the image hosting website that shows the images on the forums. If the user never archived the images they uploaded onto the image hosting sites, if those websites shut down or overhaul, their images will be lost unless the link was archived.
