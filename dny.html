Da Nhat Yen

Dạ Nhật Yến (Vũ, Thị Dạ Lộc) was born on October 12, 1978 in Hồ Chí Minh City. Her family immigrated to the United States in 1982 first to Ada, Minnesota, to various other cities until landing in San Jose, California. She never sang professionally before, but played the piano and the violin, and sang at the Lido Nightclub for fun. She was offered to sign under Asia Entertainment by Trúc Hồ through connections, but her mother refused to sign, due to her still being in school.

She signed under Lang Van Music in 1997 and moved to Southern California. She performed and released covers of various popular Vietnamese and English songs. After her contract ended in 2000, she was again offered to sign under Asia Entertainment, this time taking it. She performed and released a few albums before disappearing from the industry.
