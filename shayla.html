Shayla

Shayla (Đình, Bao Châu) was born on March 9th, 1975 in Saigon (Hồ Chí Minh City), Vietnam. Her family immigrated to the United States in 1978 after the Vietnam War and settled in Houston, Texas. Her mother, Thanh Tuyền and aunt, Sơn Tuyền were famous singers. She (allegedly) first performed in Australia in front of 2000 people, but felt inexpienced and went to pursue a career in teaching. until Trúc Hồ, a musician, invited her to sign under Asian Entertainment. She moved to Southern California to pursue her career in the music industry shortly after.

She was under Asia Entertainment from 1996-2005 and released four albums during that time. She suddenly left the industry, citing family matters.

On January 2nd, 2026, she released her first single in over 20 years, titled "Good Enough".
